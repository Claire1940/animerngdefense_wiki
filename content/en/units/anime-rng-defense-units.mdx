### Upgrade Priority Plan
During a match, you earn gold by defeating enemies. How you spend this gold determines if you survive the final waves.

1. **Primary Carry**: Upgrade your S-Tier AoE unit to at least Level 3 before focusing on others.
2. **Range Extensions**: Prioritize upgrades that increase the attack radius of your center-map units.
3. **Buffing Units**: If you have support units, only upgrade them once your main damage dealers are struggling to one-shot basic mobs.

| Strategy Component | Importance | Priority |

| **Path Coverage** | High | 1st |

| **Base Health** | Low | 3rd |
