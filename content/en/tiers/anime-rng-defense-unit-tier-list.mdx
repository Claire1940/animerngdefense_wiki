<Section icon={Map} title="Team Building and Map Strategy">

Building a successful lineup requires a strategic blend of unit types. A common mistake is filling all slots with expensive high-rarity units. While strong, these units often cost too much to deploy in the opening waves, leading to early defeats. A balanced team should feature cheap starter units to handle wave one, followed by mid-cost defenders, and finally your main carries.

Map layouts also dictate your placement strategy. Maps with winding paths and multiple U-turns offer prime locations for AoE units, as enemies will remain within their attack range for longer periods. Always analyze the enemy pathing before placing your defenders to get the maximum value out of every upgrade.
