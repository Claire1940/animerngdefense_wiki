<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Unit Stats and Rerolls">

In Anime RNG Defense, rolling a high-rarity anime-style unit is only the first step toward building an unstoppable defense lineup. To tackle the hardest maps and survive late-game enemy waves, you must optimize your unit stats. Rerolling stats allows you to replace poor random attributes with high-tier multipliers that drastically improve your unit's combat efficiency. 

Every unit has base attributes that determine their performance on the battlefield. These stats are graded from low-tier ranks (like F and E) up to elite ranks (like A, S, and potentially higher). Rerolling resets these grades randomly, giving you a chance to secure massive percentage boosts.

| Stat Name | Primary Combat Effect | Recommended Grade | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage (ATK)** | Increases raw damage dealt per hit | **S-Tier** | ★★★★★ |
| **Cooldown (CDR)** | Reduces the time between unit attacks | **A-Tier or S-Tier** | ★★★★★ |
| **Range** | Expands the attack coverage area on the map | **B-Tier or A-Tier** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Critical Rate** | Boosts the chance to land double-damage strikes | **B-Tier** | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Support Buffs** | Enhances nearby towers (specific units only) | **C-Tier or B-Tier** | ★★☆☆☆ |

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Reroll Stats: Step-by-Step">

Rerolling stats requires you to visit the upgrade area in the main lobby. Before you begin, ensure you have gathered enough Crystals and Stat Cubes. You can acquire these currencies by completing maps, surviving waves, or redeeming active promo codes.

Follow this step-by-step process to successfully reroll your unit stats:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Stat Cubes and Scrolls">
    Visit the in-game Item Store and speak with the shop NPC (such as Dr. Punk) to purchase Stat Cubes and Scrolls using your Crystals. Alternatively, redeem codes like **INNOVATIONCITY** to get a head start with free boxes and crystals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Stat Reroll NPC">
    Navigate to the Upgrade and Trait section in the main city hub. Interact with the stat-rolling pedestal or NPC to open the specialized Stat Reroll interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Target Unit">
    Choose the high-rarity unit from your inventory that you want to upgrade. Place them into the active slot to display their current stat grades (e.g., F, D, B).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock Your Best Stats">
    If your unit already has one excellent stat (such as an A-grade Cooldown), use a **Perfect Scroll** to lock that specific slot. This prevents the stat from changing during the next roll.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute the Reroll">
    Spend your Stat Cubes to roll the remaining unlocked slots. Repeat this process until you achieve a balanced, high-tier stat distribution that fits your unit's combat role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Warning">
Locking stats increases the cost of subsequent rerolls. Only lock stats that are genuinely high-grade (A-Tier or S-Tier) to avoid draining your Stat Cubes too quickly on mediocre upgrades.
</Callout>

</Section>
