<Section icon={Target} title="How to Roll and Reroll Traits">

Optimizing your unit traits requires a systematic approach to resource management and luck maximization. Follow these steps to efficiently roll and upgrade your team's traits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accumulate Crystals and Boxes">
    Collect Crystals by completing daily maps, surviving wave thresholds, and redeeming active community codes. Keep your Common, Rare, and Epic boxes sealed until you are ready for a major rolling session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Luck Boosts">
    Never roll traits or units raw. Always activate luck boosts beforehand to increase the spawn rate of S-Tier and A-Tier modifiers. Luck boosts are obtainable via code rewards, achievements, or the in-game shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Target Unit">
    Navigate to the trait rolling area in the main lobby. Place your strongest damage dealer or crowd-control unit into the selection slot. Make sure you have enough Crystals to cover multiple rerolls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Rerolls and Lock Modifiers">
    Roll for new traits. If you land a high-tier modifier like Godly or Demonic, lock the trait immediately to prevent accidental overwrites during future optimization phases.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Luck Mechanics Info">
Luck boosts do not guarantee an S-Tier trait, but they significantly shift the probability curve. Running a 2x or 3x luck boost during rolling sessions is highly recommended to avoid wasting Crystals on standard rolls.
</Callout>
