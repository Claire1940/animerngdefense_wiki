<Section icon={Swords} title="Strategic Team Building and Placements">

A successful lineup in Anime RNG Defense requires balance. Having only high-damage, slow-attacking units will leave you vulnerable to fast, low-health enemies. Conversely, having only fast-attacking, low-damage units will make it difficult to defeat bosses.

| Unit Position | Optimal Role | Target Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Frontline** | High-frequency attack / stun | First enemy in range |
| **Mid-path** | Main DPS / Area of Effect (AoE) | Strongest / Close group |
| **Backline** | Single-target sniper / Clean-up | Last / Weakest enemies |

When building your team, ensure you have at least one reliable early-game defender that can be placed cheaply in the first wave. This gives you time to save cash for upgrading your main carries as the waves scale in difficulty.

<Callout type="info" title="AoE Placement">
Always place Area of Effect (AoE) units at intersections. This allows their splash damage to hit multiple lanes simultaneously, drastically reducing the pressure on your single-target defenders.
</Callout>

</Section>
