<Section icon={Swords} title="F2P Team Building and Unit Placements">

Building a balanced team is the difference between clearing a stage and letting enemies breach your base. A common mistake is filling a lineup with high-rarity units that cost too much to place during the early waves of a match. A successful F2P team requires a mix of cheap starter units, crowd control options, and high-damage carries.

When placing units, always consider the path layout. Placing units at corners or along winding paths allows them to attack enemies for a longer duration, increasing their overall damage output.

| Role | Primary Target | Placement Priority | Upgrade Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Main Carry** | Bosses, Elites | Mid-map corners | **Highest** |
| **Wave Clear** | Enemy groups | Straight paths | **Medium** |
| **Early Control** | Fast runners | Near enemy spawns | **Low** |
| **Support Setup** | Buffing carries | Adjacent to carries | **Medium** |

Rather than focusing solely on unit rarity, look at how your units interact. A strong wave-clearing unit paired with a single-target boss killer covers all bases, ensuring that fast-moving mobs do not slip past while your main damage dealer focuses on high-health targets.
